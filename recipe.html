Egusi Recipe

Ingredients

  • Egusi melon
  • Palm oil
  • Beef, dried fish, and crayfish
  • Locust beans
  • Spinach
  • Pepper mix
  • Onions
  • Salt
  • Stock cube
  • Beef stock

Preparation steps

Step 1: Blend the peppers and onions together

Step 2: Blend the Egusi melon

Step 3: Heat the palm oil but don't overheat

Step 4: Add onions and locust beans

Step 5: Add the blended pepper

Step 6: Add the remaining locust beans, beef stock, and salt to taste. Cover and leave for 5 minutes

Tips and Variations

Tip 1: If you are a vegetarian, you can still eat Egusi soup. Omit the meat and beef broth. You can replace it with vegetable broth.

Tip 2: If you are worried about calories from palm oil, you can reduce the quantity you use.

Tip 3: Use as many leafy vegetables as you like; there is no limit to the quantity.

Tip 4: Blend the pepper with little water. Avoid watery pepper mix because too much water will prolong the cooking time of the sauce

Variation: Spinach was used in the making of this recipe but any leafy greens can be used as a substitute
